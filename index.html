Got Questions

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

Can I cancel my membership at any time? +
Monthly memberships can be cancelled with 30 days notice — no cancellation fees ever. Annual memberships are pre-paid but include a 1-month freeze benefit per year.
What are your opening hours? +
Elite and Champion members enjoy 24/7 access. Starter members can access the gym from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Staffed hours are 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM weekdays, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM weekends.
How do I book a class or training session? +
Classes can be booked via our member app (iOS & Android) or through the schedule on this website. Personal training sessions can be booked directly with your coach through the app.
Is there a free trial available? +
Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial that includes full gym access, two complimentary group classes, and a fitness assessment with one of our coaches. No credit card required to start.
Do you offer corporate or student discounts? +
We offer 15% corporate rates for companies with 10+ employees and 20% student discounts with valid ID. Contact us directly to set up a corporate account or verify student eligibility.
What equipment does the gym have? +
Our facility features 200+ cardio machines, full Olympic lifting platforms, extensive free weights (up to 200lbs), cable systems, functional training rigs, and a dedicated rehabilitation area. All equipment is replaced on a 5-year cycle.
